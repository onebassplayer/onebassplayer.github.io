Let's Talk What I Do

About Me

I'm the founder of Moonshot Consulting, a fractional recruiting consultancy that specializes in talent acquisition for startups, growth-stage companies, and federal contractors. I also work as a Strategic Talent Partner at Revolution Venture Studios in NYC, where I recruit co-founders and founding engineers for portfolio companies.

With over 15 years of recruiting experience across companies like Confluent, Splunk, and The Trade Desk, I've learned that the best hiring happens when you treat people like people, not pipeline metrics.

When I'm not recruiting, I'm a performing bass guitarist playing across R&B, funk, rock, jazz, and gospel genres. I bring the same "listen first, play second" philosophy to both music and talent work.
